# East Coast Swing Syllabus

## ECS Bronze 1-5

- turning basic right
- turning basic left
- throw out
- inside underarm turn


## ECS Bronze 6-10

- underarm release from basic
- free-spin tuck-in from handshake
- underarm tuck-in
- alternating underarm turn
- shoulder check
- cradle

## ECS Bronze 11-15

- cradle to hammer-lock
- sugar-push throw out
- double face loop
- opposition break and roll out
- whirlpool
